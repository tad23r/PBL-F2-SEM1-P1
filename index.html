What is GeoShift?

GeoShift is an innovative puzzle game designed for players who crave intellectual challenges with fresh mechanics. Our game combines simple-to-learn but hard-to-master shape-shifting mechanics with increasingly complex puzzles that will test your spatial reasoning and problem-solving skills.

Shape-Shifting Mechanics

Players can morph between different shapes to pass through specific barriers.
