Game Controls

Movement

Use AD to move your character left and right.

Special Ability

Press F to to use a special ability.

Jump

Press Space or W to jump

Game Tips

1

Everything is Unique!

Each form has unique abilities. The circle can phase through wall, the triangle can double-jump, the pentagon can walk over spikes and the star can teleport. Try everything!

2

Be Aware...

Pay attention to the door knob on each door. They often indicate which shape you'll need to solve upcoming puzzles.

3

Dont Stress Out!

Some puzzles require rapid shape-shifting. It has switching forms mid-air or while moving to overcome complex obstacles.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the control of this game?

You can look up the controls on either the settings in the game homepage or at the start of this page.

Why can't I use my special ability on some shapes?

Certain shapes doesn't have special ability, but they do have special properties. For example, pentagon can walk over spikes and triangle can double jumps.

Can I play on mobile devices?

No, even though it is responsive on some mobile devices, it can't be played on mobile devices.

How many levels does the game have?

The current version has 3 levels for now, but there will be more levels in updates.

Is this game suitable for me?

This game features puzzle and need critical thinking. Even if you don't like puzzle, this game can improve your brains' way of thinking.
